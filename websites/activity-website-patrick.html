HYBRID CARS

Hybrid cars, also known as Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs), combine a gasoline engine with an electric motor and battery, allowing for more fuel efficiency and reduced emissions compared to traditional gasoline-only vehicles. .

Hybrid Cars

Hybrid cars use both a gasoline engine and an electric motor to power the vehicle..

Hybrid Now

The electric motor assists the gasoline engine, particularly during acceleration and at lower speeds, improving fuel economy.

More Powerfull

There are different types of hybrid vehicles, including mild hybrids (which use a smaller battery and electric motor), full hybrids (which can run solely on electric power for short distances), and plug-in hybrids (which can be recharged via an external power source).

battery service

A battery pack stores the electricity for the electric motor, which is recharged through regenerative braking and sometimes through plugging into an external power source (in plug-in hybrid vehicles).
